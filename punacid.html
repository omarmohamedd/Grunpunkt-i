PUNACID

Punacid is an advanced formulation designed to have an extra-long acidifying action inside the digestive tract of broilers, layers and turkey,Punacid is combination of the Short Chain Fatty Acids (SCFA)- formic-, lactic-, acetic- and citric acids and their calcium salts with Medium Chain Fatty Acids (MCFA) enriched in Lauric acid (C12:0) to expand its range of functionalities. The use of advanced protection technology,enables the birds to benefit from 100% of the components of Punacid, In addition, it increases the palatability of the ration, and to improve the digestion and absorption of the feedstuffs, Punacid indirectly controls infections due to respiratory complex, respiratory stress, wet litter and coccidiosis in the farm. It also increases eggshell thickness and egg hatchability.
