Toxins Management

This range of products combine 3 different modes of action (Adsorption, Advanced cell protection, and Biotransformation) developed to help to mitigate problems associated with the potentially harmful effects of a wide range of mycotoxins
found in the feed of livestock and aquaculture.

Myconix
  • Protects against polar and non-polar mycotoxins.
  • Supports liver function by herbal extract content.
  • Synergistic mixture mycotoxins binders.
Mycolect
  • Highly efficient in binding aflatoxin (feed safety).
  • Reduces AFM1 secretion in milk (food safety).
  • Activated clays as mycotoxins binders.
