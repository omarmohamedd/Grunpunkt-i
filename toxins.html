Toxins Management

This range of products combine 3 different modes of action (adsorption, advanced cell protection, and Biotransformation) developed to help to mitigate problems associated with the potentially harmful effects of a wide range of mycotoxins found in the feed of livestock and aquaculture.

Myconix

Synergistic mixture mycotoxins binders

Supports liver function by herbal extract content

Protects against polar and non-polar mycotoxins

Mycolect

Activated clays as mycotoxins binders

Highly efficient in binding aflatoxin (feed safety)

Reduces AFM1 secretion in milk (food safety)
