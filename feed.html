Feed Efficiency Management

This group of products is concerned about feed hygiene, high performance and efficiency, thus has a huge impact on the
general health of birds, their production efficiency,
food safety, and environment.

Grüzym
  • Endo-1,4-beta-xylanase enzyme.
  • NSP Multi-enzyme.
  • 6-phytase enzyme.
G-Bond
  • Low inclusion flexible pellet binder for a wide range of feed types.
  • Natural anti-caking to assure feed homogeneity.
  • Natural binders with a synergistic effect.
  • Safe and efficient even in small doses.
  • Improves pellet quality and durability.
Lipigrün
  • Reduces feed mills energy consumption and maintains their infrastructure.
  • Reduces the feed cost and increases profit of feed mills.
  • Helps young animals to improve the digestibility of fats.
  • Protected bio-surfactants and bio-emulsifiers.
  • Improves feed processing and feed quality.
  • Improves feed digestion and assimilation.
  • Rich in lysophospholipids.
Punacid
  • Prevents the development of moulds, fungi, and bacteria in feed mixtures and TMR.
  • Secures longer feed storage, silages, and grains.
  • Protected organic and inorganic acids.
Radinix
  • Maintains the freshness of feed and stabilizing its nutritive value.
  • Affects the 3 phases of fatty acids oxidation.
  • Synergistic mixture of natural antioxidants.
  • Sustains feed shelf life.
Grünetra
  • Stimulates immunity and enhances growth.
  • Garlic extract and yeast cell wall.
  • Natural growth promoter (NGP).
  • Extra prebiotic content.
  • Hepato-protective.
OsOvO
  • Improves and maintains high laying yields.
  • Maintains HU quality during storage.
  • Egg shell and albumin enhancer.
  • Improves mineral formulation.
