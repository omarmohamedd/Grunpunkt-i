Grünpunkt : Four Areas of Expertise

Maximizing the average daily gains of your beef cattle at the lowest input cost is always difficult. To overcome this challenge, we stand beside you and offer the long experience and knowhow of our team in Grünpunkt, our partner researchers, collaborators, advisors and nutritionists. Grünpunkt delivers carefully selected products. Our products are designed to cover a wide range of needs and to meet various nutritional requirements of your herds in order to improve their health and maximize your profitability. The core of our interest is to deliver innovative solutions more efficiently and more economically. We are grateful for all our partners in more than 23 countries who gave us the opportunity to stand behind their animals. In addition, Grünpunkt offers sustainability and flexibility, even customized products and individual solutions are also available to support our customers worldwide.
