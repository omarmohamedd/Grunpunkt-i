About Us

We work in a holistic manner in multiple facets for animal well-being. We take pride in being able to keep your animals healthy
and productive through our quality product. Grünpunkt products are developed and constantly improved through partnerships.
Our expertise and partnerships help us constantly to improve, innovate and impart. Grünpunkt portfolio is divided into five categories:
feed efficiency management, toxin management, gut health management, vets solutions, raw materials, and premixes.
