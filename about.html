About Us

We work in a holistic manner in multiple facets for animal well-being. We take pride in
being able to keep your animals healthy and productive through our quality product.
Grünpunkt products are developed and constantly improved through partnerships.
Our expertise and partnerships help us constantly to improve, innovate and impart.
Grünpunkt portfolio is divided into five categories:
Feed efficiency management, Toxin management,
Gut health management, Vets solutions,
Raw materials, and Premixes.

Our Commitment

Grünpunkt products come in specially designed packaging to guarantee consistency of the product and an excellent shelf life.
Grünpunkt cutting-edge feed additive products work in harmony with the natural aspects of both the animal biology
and environment to optimize health, growth, and profitability.
