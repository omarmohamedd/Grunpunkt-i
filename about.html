About Us

Grünpunkt name is synonymous to Green Point where all our team are working hard to innovate feed additives and nutritional
concepts comply with and preserve healthy environment.

We have also a strict commitment for developing high performance natural feed additives and sustainable solutions that ensure
our customers’ success through healthy and safe animal nutrition.

Grünpunkt team are using science and expertise to understand and appreciate our customer’s needs and concerns. This Approach
enables us to provide solutions that support animal health, high performance and production efficiency.

Grünpunkt supplies its customers with quality assured products manufactured in Germany and understanding the needs of most
markets around the world.
